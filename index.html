Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LoyWallet

How much does LoyWallet cost?

LoyWallet is completely free! Store unlimited loyalty cards, use all features, and enjoy the full experience at no cost. We believe everyone should have access to better loyalty card management without paying for it.

How secure is my card data?

Your data is protected with industry-standard encryption. We never store your full card numbers, and all data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. You can also enable biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) for an extra layer of security.

Does LoyWallet work offline?

Absolutely! All your cards are stored locally on your device, so you can access them anytime without an internet connection. You only need internet to initially add cards or sync across devices.

Can I use LoyWallet on multiple devices?

Yes! Your cards automatically sync across all your devices when you sign in. You can use LoyWallet on both iPhone and Android devices.

What if a store doesn't accept digital cards?

LoyWallet displays your loyalty card barcode exactly as it appears on your physical card. Over 99% of stores with barcode scanners can read digital barcodes from your phone screen. If a cashier is unsure, you can simply tell them your loyalty number which is also displayed on your card.

How do I add a card that won't scan?

If auto-scan doesn't work, you can manually enter your card number. Just select the store from our database of 20,000+ retailers, enter your loyalty number, and we'll generate the correct barcode format for you.

Can I share cards with family members?

Yes! With Family Sharing, you can share household loyalty cards with up to 6 family members. Everyone can access the shared cards from their own device and earn points together. Perfect for maximizing rewards on shared purchases.

Does LoyWallet drain my battery?

No! We've optimized LoyWallet for minimal battery usage. Location reminders use geofencing technology which is extremely battery-efficient. Most users report less than 1% battery drain per day even with all features enabled.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

1. Introduction

LoyWallet ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and website (loywallet.io). We are fully committed to GDPR compliance for our users in the European Union.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address, full name, and username provided during signup.
  • Wallet Data: Loyalty card names, barcode numbers, and optional card images.
  • Authentication: We use Supabase and Google OAuth for secure login.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the LoyWallet service. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We use your data to:

  • Sync your loyalty cards across devices.
  • Generate digital barcodes for scanning.
  • Secure your account.

4. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can see all your data within the app settings.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You can delete your account permanently via the "Delete Account" button in your profile. This will instantly wipe all your data from our servers.
  • Right to Portability: You can request a copy of your stored data in a structured format.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard encryption, rate-limiting, and CORS whitelisting to protect your data from unauthorized access.
